The Piggy Market

Artisan Delicatessen & Craft Butchers

  • Local

    The Piggy Market strives to support a local food system by getting to know and developing relationships with the local area's farmers.

  • Gourmet

    Whether you're looking for a quality bite for lunch, something gourmet for dinner, or a butcher cut to prepare your favourite meal, we have what you need.

  • Organic

    The Piggy Market supplies only 100% organic, local, and free run meat. This guarantees the best quality meat possible, as well as best tasting.

  • Seasonal

    Almost everything that comes out of our kitchen is seasonal, as it is grown or raised locally: from butcher cuts, deli meats, cheeses to a range of prepared foods.
